Meril LIS Agent
LAB QUICK GUIDE

FOR RUNNING A SAMPLE

Step-by-step workflow for lab technicians
1
Open LIS-Agent App
Launch the app — it auto-connects to all configured analyzers
💻
2
Go to Worklist
View all pending tests assigned by the doctor
📋
3
Find the Patient Sample
Search by name, sample ID, or scan barcode label
🔍
4
Click "Run Test"
Links the next analyzer result to this sample automatically
▶️
5
Load Sample on Analyzer
Place the tube, aspirate, and press the start / aspiration button
🧪
6
Wait — Results Auto-Upload
LIS-Agent captures result via serial/TCP and sends to cloud
⏳
7
Verify Results
Doctor reviews values, edits if needed, then approves
✅
8
Print / Download Report
Finalized PDF report is ready to share with the patient
🖨️
Keep this app running at all times.
Results upload automatically — no manual sync needed.
